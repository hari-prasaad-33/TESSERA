## Slide 2: Problem

**Mixing kills creative flow.**

- Producers and engineers spend hours tweaking EQs, compressors, and dynamics
- Technical decisions interrupt the creative process
- Steep learning curve for amateurs; repetitive work for professionals
- AI tools exist for mastering and stems—but not for the *channel strip*, where most mix decisions happen

---

## Slide 3: Solution

**Tessera One: An intelligent channel strip that listens, analyzes, and suggests.**

- Describe what you want in plain language: *"warmer and punchier," "more presence," "tighter low end"*
- AI analyzes your audio (8-second capture) and returns parameter suggestions in seconds
- One-click apply—or tweak manually. You stay in control.
- Not a replacement for the engineer. A catalyst for faster, more confident mixes.

---

## Slide 4: Product Demo

**How it works:**

1. Load audio in your DAW (or standalone)
2. Type a goal in natural language
3. Hit Analyze → AI captures 8 seconds, runs spectral analysis
4. Review suggested EQ, dynamics, saturation, reverb
5. Apply with one click or adjust by hand

**Full channel strip:** Gate → 8-Band EQ → Compressor → Saturation → Reverb → Limiter

## Slide 5: Technology

- **AI:** OpenAI GPT-4/4o or Google Gemini—flexible, best-in-class models

- **Formats:** VST3, AU, Standalone (Windows + macOS)
